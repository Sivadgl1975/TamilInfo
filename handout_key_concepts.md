# Handout: Key Concepts from AI & Context Engineering Master Presentation

## Introduction
This handout summarizes the key concepts from the AI & Context Engineering Master Presentation. Use it as a reference during and after the session.

## Part I: Understanding AI
- **AI Definition:** Simulation of human intelligence in machines.
- **Types:** Narrow AI (task-specific) vs. General AI (human-like).
- **Key Components:** Data, algorithms, computing power.
- **AI Literacy:** Understanding applications, evaluating outputs, ethical use.
- **Model Types:** Supervised, unsupervised, reinforcement, deep learning.
- **AI Process:** Data collection → preprocessing → training → validation → deployment.

## Part II: AI Failure Modes
- **Common Failures:** Hallucinations, bias, overfitting, adversarial attacks.
- **Cognitive Debt:** Accumulation of unresolved AI issues.
- **Privacy Spectrum:** From data collection to user rights (GDPR, CCPA).

## Part III: Knowledge Management
- **Processes:** Capture, store, retrieve, share, update knowledge.
- **ROI Metrics:** Time saved, productivity, innovation, cost savings.
- **PKM AI-Ready:** Digital tools, AI summarization, knowledge graphs.

## Part IV: Context Engineering
- **Definition:** Managing surrounding information for better AI.
- **Properties:** Relevance, accuracy, timeliness, completeness.
- **Problems & Solutions:** Ambiguity, incompleteness, scalability issues.
- **Implementation:** Requirements gathering, modeling, deployment.

## Part V: AI Agents
- **Definition:** Autonomous systems with perception and action.
- **Anatomy:** Sensors, actuators, reasoning, knowledge base.
- **Multi-Agent Systems:** Coordination, communication, fault tolerance.
- **Failure Modes:** Technical, security, ethical issues.

## Part VI: Skill & Agent Engineering
- **Building Skills:** Requirements, design, testing, deployment.
- **Distribution:** Cloud, on-premise, APIs, marketplaces.
- **Security:** Threat modeling, encryption, compliance.

## Part VII: Context at Every Level
- **Master Prompt:** Structured prompts for AI interaction.
- **Frameworks:** PCM (Personal), TCM (Team), ECM (Enterprise) Context Management.
- **Adoption Roadmap:** Assessment, planning, implementation, monitoring.
- **Governance:** Policies, ethics, compliance, leadership.

## Part VIII: Strategy & Future
- **Safety & Ethics:** Principles, governance, regulations.
- **Agentic Future:** Societal changes, economic impact, challenges.

## Appendix
